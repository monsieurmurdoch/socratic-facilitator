What we run

A small-group intellectual extracurricular.

Human-led facilitation

Students meet in recurring discussion pods led by trained facilitators who know how to keep rigor, warmth, and momentum in the room.

Plato inside the room

Our AI facilitator tracks the conversation, notices quiet voices and strong ideas, and asks careful questions without taking over.

After-session intelligence

Teachers and families get a useful record of what happened: transcript, highlights, discussion quality, and next-session notes.

How it works

Built for recurring seminars, not one-off AI demos.

  1. Choose the text.A poem, essay, story, primary source, or philosophical question.
  2. Gather the pod.Usually 5-8 students with a recurring weekly rhythm.
  3. Run the room.A human facilitator leads. Plato supports by asking, noticing, and remembering.
  4. Review the trace.Session analytics show who spoke, what ideas carried weight, and where to go next.

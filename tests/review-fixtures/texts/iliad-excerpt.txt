1. Sing, goddess, the anger of Achilles son of Peleus,
2. that brought countless ills upon the Achaeans.
3. Many a brave soul did it send hurrying down to Hades,
4. and many a hero did it yield a prey to dogs and vultures,
5. for so were the counsels of Zeus fulfilled.
6. Begin, Muse, when the two first broke and clashed,
7. Agamemnon lord of men and brilliant Achilles.

# Human Smoke Checklist

## Plato quality
- Start a 1:1 warmup and give Plato a tentative answer. Verify Plato asks a clarifying or evidence-seeking question instead of summarizing the "right" interpretation.
- Paste a short numbered passage and ask Plato about a specific line. Verify he either cites the visible numbering or admits he cannot locate it.

## Text reading
- Upload a born-digital PDF and confirm the dashboard source-text viewer shows stable canonical lines.
- Upload a scanned/image-only PDF and confirm the app either extracts readable text via OCR or clearly reports OCR is unavailable.

## Screenshare / room flow
- Join a room with no live session and confirm the waiting state is coherent.
- Start a session from the room and verify the dashboard opens with transcript, participants, and source text all visible.

## Prompt / grounding
- Ask Plato about a phrase that does not appear in the source text. Verify he does not bluff a line citation.
- Ask Plato to connect two visible lines. Verify the answer stays grounded in those lines rather than drifting to a generic summary.
